Adolescents

Life Teen is a youth program centered in Christ and for Christ. Our mission is to lead teens closer to Christ, make his love, grace, and mercy known; while preparing them to receive the sacrament of confirmation.

Life teen is composed by the following groups:

*Catechist, are adults over the age of 21 who introduce and guide the youth in their journey to being confirmed and becoming disciples of the living Christ.

*Core Team, are young adults over the age of 18 who are committed to aide and guide teens that have received the sacrament of confirmation, in living a life focused in Christ.

Requirements:

*Must have CMG Connect.

*Must have been baptized, received Holy Eucharist, and Confirmation Sacraments.

*Militia are the youth who have received the Sacrament of Confirmation and have chosen to answer the call to follow Jesus. They are committed, hardworking, dedicated, and eager to lead their fellow teens who are preparing to receive the Sacrament of Confirmation closer to Christ.

What is Confirmation?

The preparation for the sacrament of Confirmation is an important component of our catechetical and sacramental ministry.

Confirmation is one of the three sacraments of initiation of the Catholic Church, along with Baptism and Eucharist. Confirmation will unleash the Holy Spirit in your life, just as it did on the apostles at Pentecost. In this way Confirmation perpetuates Pentecost in the Church, so that the work of Jesus can be continued in every age. This sacrament, which provides the fullness of the gifts of the Holy Spirit, is of great spiritual assistance to our youth during this time in their lives.

Our Lady of Fatima has a two-year preparation that starts at the age of 15. We have several local retreats throughout the course of the two years. Our teens conclude their preparation with one retreat out of town that starts of Friday, and concludes with mass on Sunday.
