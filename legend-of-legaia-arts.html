4. Classify the following art forms. Use the legends below:Classification of Arts.BA - business artPA - practical artCA - civic art,MA medical artAA - agricultural ArtIA - industrial artPA - practical artFA - fishery artGA- graphic artCA - commercial art​


1. Medical art

The medical arts cover a wide range of applications from patient communication and information to medical teaching and training.It is also used by the pharmaceutical industry to help explain their products and in court rooms to help jury members understand complex topics.Medical artists produce work in a variety of media including traditional illustration, as well as interactive 3D models, and animation.

2. Civic art

Civic art is the sum total of the architecture, public spaces, monuments, urban design, and landscape of a city, but it is far more than the sum of the parts.

3. Graphic art

Graphic art is a branch of art whose work is made using printing techniques, usually on paper. Except for the Monotype technique, the process is able to create many copies of the same work, this is called the printing process. Each copy of the work is known as an 'impression'.

4. Business art

Art Business Info for artists is a compendium of advice, information and resources for artists about different aspects of the art business.

5. Practical art

Practical works of art are works of art whose main purpose is intended as disposable objects, for example, household furniture, tables, chairs and textiles.

6. Agriculture art

Agricultural arts are taught at primary and secondary schools and colleges across the country, especially in agricultural regions.

7. Industrial art

Industrial arts is an educational program that features the fabrication of objects in wood or metal using a variety of hand, power, or machine tools

8. Fishery art

Agri-fishery Arts strand is one of the programs under the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood track in senior high school. This strand is designed to provide students with adequate knowledge and skills relating to agriculture and aquaculture

9. Commercial art

Commercial art which means art whose main purpose is to produce works for sale.

10. Applied art

Applied art is a work of art used in everyday life which contains certain functional values ​​in addition to its artistic value. The function of works of art can be divided into two, namely the aesthetic function and the practical function.

10. How is the Turban Legend is titled "Turban Legend"?


Urban legends are modern forms of folklore formed within the context of modern society. 

A turban legend, refers to a person of Middle Eastern descent who displays a type of "legendary" comprehension of subject matters. People who live in the Middle East are known for their use of the turban which they wrap around their head, thus the term "Turban legend"
