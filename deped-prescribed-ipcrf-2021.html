4. Prescribed connector


Answer:

The ISO 80369 and ISO 18250 series of standards were introduced to prevent accidental mis-connections between different medical connectors. The prevention of mis-connection is achieved through the specification of the different types of small-bore connectors and reservoir connectors so that they are purposely dimensionally incompatible. This must be proven through CAD-analysis and/or physical testing.

There are currently eight standards in the ISO 80369 series, of which six are currently published and one is underway. The eighth standard has been in development for urethral and urinary applications for some time, although development of this standard has stalled.

While developing the ISO 80369 series it became apparent that mis-connections on the patient side were not the only ones possible. The ISO 18250 series was therefore developed to cover reservoir connectors and assist in reducing risk of mis-connection between small-bore connectors and reservoir connectors. This series contains five standards, and all are published.
