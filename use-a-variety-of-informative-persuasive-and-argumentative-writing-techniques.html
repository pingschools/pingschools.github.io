12. What i have learned in using a variety of informative, analytical, persuasive and argumentative writing techniques​


Answer:

Informative Essay

This type of essay explains a short theme, idea, or an issue. It is a personal response to anything and is usually three paragraphs long.

Analytical Essay

This type of essay analyzes, interprets, or examines an art work, a film, a literary work, a song, a composition, or a media text. It is usually written in four parts:

a. the introduction tells you the what text you will be discussing and why;

b. the textual analysis which is closely related to your main argument or idea in the introduction;

c. your personal reading of the text that must show critically; and,

d. the conclusion which shows the connection between your argument and the text.

Persuasive Essay

This type of essay convinces a reader to believe or adopt a viewpoint on a certain issue. It is sometimes written in three to five paragraphs.

Argumentative Essay

This type of essay argues in a sense that it proves a hypothesis, a proposition, a theory, or an opinion to an opposite hypothesis, propositon, theory, idea, or opinion. It is usually writen in five paragraphs to show the pros and cons with claims and subpoints for each.

Answer:

1. An informative essay is a paper that provides information on a specific topic. In other words, it educates the reader about a particular thing, be it a term, event, natural phenomenon, etc. An informative essay doesn't contain the author's evaluation or analysis. Instead, it aims to describe a subject.

2. An analytical essay is a piece of writing that provides substantive analysis of a topic. Analysis papers can be written about art, music, literary works, current events, historical events, politics, scientific research, and philosophy, among other topics.

3. Persuasive writing, also known as the argument essay, uses logic and reason to show that one idea is more legitimate than another. It attempts to persuade a reader to adopt a certain point of view or to take a particular action.

4. An argumentative essay is a piece of writing that takes a stance on an issue. In a good argumentative essay, a writer attempts to persuade readers to understand and support their point of view about a topic by stating their reasoning and providing evidence to back it up
