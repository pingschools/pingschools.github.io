22. edna bought an RTW dress for Php575.00 at 20 discount. what was the original price?


Answer

The original price would be 718.75, to check:

Discount amount = 718.75 * .2 (.2 is 20% discount converted to decimals)

Discount amount = 143.75 (20% of 718.75)

Apply the Discount amount

D i s c o u n t e d  Price = 718.75 - 143.75

D i s c o u n t e d  Price = 575

To get the original price, use Reverse Percentage.

Explanation

We are looking for X, which is X = original price

So, the problem we are solving is, a 20% decrease of X is 575.

Since the percent type is a decrease(discount), we subtract 20% from 100%.

So, the D i s c o u n t e d  Price is 100%-20% = 80%. Therefore, 80% of X is 575.

This leads to the equation:

0.8 * X = 575

X = 575 / 0.8

X = 718.75.
