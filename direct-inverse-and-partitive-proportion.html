6. what is inverse proportion,direct proportion,and partitive proportion? Answer pls...


Answer:

INVERSE PROPORTION

two quantities are inversely proportional if an increase in one quantity corresponds to a constant decrease in the other quantity,or if a decrease in one quantity corresponds to a constant increase in the other quantity.

DIRECT PROPORTION

two values x and y are directly proportional to each other when x and y will either increase or decrease together,such that one when increases,the other increases or when one decreases,the other.

PARTITIVE PROPORTION

a partitive PROPORTION of proportion by parts, describe a total amount being distributed into two or more unequal parts.

Solution using the property on proportion.

Step 1: Set up the proportion. Make sure that the order of terms in the first ratio is the same as the order of terms in the second ratio. Represent the number of tetra packs of green iced tea by n.

red iced tea:green iced tea = red iced tea:green iced tea

5:7 = 45:n

Step 2: Solve for the missing term by using the principle on ratio and proportion. The product of the means equals the product of the extremes.
5:7 = 45:n
5 x n = 7 x 45
5n = 315
