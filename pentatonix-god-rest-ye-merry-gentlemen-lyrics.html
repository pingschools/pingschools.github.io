1. What is the meter of God Rest Ye' Merry Gentlemen?​


Answer:

Compound meter (6/8)

Explanation:

6/8 time has also two quarter note beats per bar so its "duple meter" but each one divides into 3 making it a "compound meter" time signature.

24. How many ways can a committee composed of 3 ladies and 2 gentlemen be chosen from 7 ladies and 5 gentlemen?


Answer:

350 ways

Step-by-step explanation:

Out of 7 men, 3 men can be chosen in 7C3 ways and out of 5 women, 2 women can be chosen in 5C2 ways. Hence, the committee can be chosen in 7C3×5C2=350 ways
