16. What is the message of the Chinese folk song "mo li hua"​.


Answer:

The Chinese folk song "Mo Li Hua" is a love song that tells the story of a woman who is waiting for her lover to return. The song is a symbol of love, longing, and patience. The message of the song is that true love is worth waiting for, and that it can overcome any obstacle or challenge. The woman in the song patiently waits for her lover to return, even though she knows that it may be a long time before they are reunited. In the end, her love and devotion are rewarded when her lover finally returns.
