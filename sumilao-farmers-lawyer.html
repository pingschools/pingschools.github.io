Answer:

This quote is emphasizing the importance of farmers in our everyday lives. Without farmers, it would be very difficult to get food and other agricultural goods. The quote is pointing out the fact that while we may need a doctor, lawyer, or policeman on occasion, we need a farmer every single day. Farmers are the ones who provide us with food to eat three times a day, so without them, our lives would be very different. This quote is a reminder of how important farmers are and how much we rely on them for our everyday sustenance.
