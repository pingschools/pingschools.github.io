2. Dream vacation essay with cohesive device


My ideal vacation Hello, everyone. I'd like to tell you about my ideal vacation. To make my vacation perfect, certain important things need to be taken into consideration. These things include who I spend my vacation with, where I want to go, what I want to do, and the sort of weather the place has. It can be an ideal vacation when all these things are met. First of all, I like to spend my vacation with people I love and I feel comfortable with. They could be my family, friends or even my future boy friend. Secondly, I love traveling to other countries. I love visiting new places because I can experience different cultures and meet different people who I wouldn't normally get to meet. When I go abroad, I really get the feeling that I'm living in a big world and that all my worries are so small and pointless. It would be great to go to a country I have never been to before. I've heard from many people who've been to Europe that Barcelona was the best place they'd ever visited. Barcelona is a city that has beautiful beaches and is known for having sunny and clear weather, so if I had a chance of going to any country I wanted to, I'd choose Barcelona as my ideal vacation location. On my vacation, I'd like to relax on a beautiful blue beach. I want to sunbathe and get a tan, too. Most people think of this sort image in their heads when they come up with their idea of an ideal vacation, and I agree with them. I know it's because the media influences people's ideas. But, I've always wanted to experience a vacation like that at least once in my life.

11. essay about Christmas vacation? example ​


My Essay About Christmas Vacation

My last Christmas Vacation was considered to be the best of all. I have to spend the holidays with my family. We normally doesn't get a chance to stay together since we are busy to our personal, career, academic lives. I have two siblings whom both are working already. My parents are handling a family business. Seeing them all present for the holiday is a huge thing for me. We started our day with a mass. After we said thanks to our Lord, we directly go to our house to prepare for the Christmas Eve. Me and my sister accompanied our Mom to the market and shop for the ingredients she needed for the foods. Dad and my brother stayed at home to bring up Christmas decors.  

Night time, everybody is at the table. We ate together. Everybody has their own personal stories to share while we eat. Mostly about their lives for the year. Mom shared how the business is growing. Sister shared about her work and brother shared how he is becoming good when it comes to his new hobby - biking. He wanted me to join him if only I have a bike. We exchanged gifts for each member. I got a new shirt and a cellphone. Mom had a new rice cooker. Dad, he got a shaver. While bro have a new bike helmet. This is the best Christmas ever.
