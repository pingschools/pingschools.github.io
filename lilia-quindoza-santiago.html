13. Lilia has 2 3/5 of silk cloth. After using a piece of it, she still has 1 4/5 meter of cloth left. How long of cloth did Lilia use?.


Answer:

4/5

Step-by-step explanation:

Convert the mixed numbers to an improper fraction

2 3/5 = 13/5

1 4/5 = 9/5

Subtract the fractions

13/5 - 9/5

4/5
