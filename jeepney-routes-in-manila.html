The function f is one to one if for any x1, x2 in the domain of f, then f(x1) is not equal to f(x2). That is, the same y-value is never paired with two different x-values.

Example 1: The relation pairing an SSS member to his/her SSS number.

Solution:

Each SSS member is assigned to a unique SSS number. Thus, the function is one-to-one function.

Example 2:

The relation pairing a real number to its square.

Solution:

Each real number has a unique perfect square. Thus, the relation is a function. However, two different real numbers such as 2 and -2 may have the same square. Thus, the function is one-to-one.

Example 3:

Answer:

The only one-to-one function is the relations pairing a distance d (in kilometers) traveled along a given jeepney route to the jeepney fare for traveling that distance.​

Step-by-step explanation:

What is a one-to-one function?

It is a function wherein every value substituted for x has an equal value expressed as y.

If the value of x can be equal to several values of y, it is not a one-to-one function.

So, looking at the two choices, only the relation between distance and jeepney fare is one-to-one. This is because whenever the value of distance changes, the fare also changes proportionally.

Unlike the relation of pairing a person, a person may be paired with anyone and it will not affect the value of either person. One person may also be paired with more than one person.

19. Make an essay about the impact of Jeepney Phaseout/Modernization in cultural tourism of Metro Manila. Discuss the pro's and con's.


Jeepney phase-out

    Modernizing the jeepneys is the appropriate move by the administration. Transportation modernisation is unquestionably required if, by its own description, a contemporary transport is "roadworthy, safe, and reliable" and, most all, "prevents pollution" or has "emissions within acceptable levels." Modernizing the transportation system offers advantages, no doubt. This new replacement market will be welcomed by car dealers, manufacturers, and financiers as it will result in huge increases in business. However, there are advantages and disadvantages to the jeepney phase-out, particularly when we consider one of the key stakeholders: the drivers. The ability to increase employment across the nation is one of the main benefits of modernizing the jeepney. The modernization of the jeepney could increase the demand for qualified operators and drivers. The necessity for newer models has increased the demand for workers and laborers in the automotive industry. Due to their high carbon emissions, older jeepney models are frequently found to be in violation of the Clean Air Act.

    Modernizing jeepneys is one of the initiatives that the administration thinks is appropriate as the nation shifts to a more environmentally friendly perspective. A considerable reduction in carbon emissions is anticipated with newer units. The jeepney phaseout is a persistent problem that has an impact on many areas of the nation. Stakeholders, from commuters to jeepney drivers, have discussed the issue's apparent benefits and drawbacks. Many professionals and citizens continue to offer their opinions on how transport modernization might be accomplished more effectively, despite the government's decision to put this on hold.
