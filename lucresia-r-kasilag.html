1. The life of Lucresia R. Kasilag (Summary)


Lucrecia R. Kasilag was born on the 31st of August, 1918 in San Fernando, La Union. She is the third of six children of Marcial Kasilag Sr., a government engineer, and Asuncion Roces, a music teacher, Lucrecia's first musical influence. ... At an early age, Lucrecia learnt to play the guitar.
