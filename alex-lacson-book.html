17. the discount price of a book is 20% less than the retail price. alex manages to purchase the book at 30% of the discount price ar a special book sale. what percent of the retail price alex pay?


Answer:

Let's assume the retail price of the book to be "x".

According to the question, the discount price of the book is 20% less than the retail price, which means the discount price is:

Discount price = x - 0.20x

Discount price = 0.80x

Now, Alex manages to purchase the book at 30% of the discount price, which means he pays:

30% of discount price = 0.30(0.80x) = 0.24x

Therefore, Alex pays 24% of the retail price for the book.

28. Alex spent ₱270 to buy book,a notebook, and a ballpen.The book cost ₱140 more than the notebook,while the book and the notebook cost ₱210 more than the ballpen.How muchdid he spend for a notebook?GIVE THE ANSWER AND THE SOLUTION......


Ballpen: x

Book: y

Notebook: z

x + y + z = 270

The book cost ₱140 more than the notebook:

y = z + 140

x + y + z = 270

x + (z+140) + z = 270

x + 2z + 140 = 270

x = 270 - 140 - 2z

x = 130 - 2z

The book and the notebook cost ₱210 more than the ballpen.

Find the cost of a notebook, z:

y + z = x + 210

(z + 140) + z = (130 - 2z) + 210

2z + 2z = 130 + 210 - 140

4z/4 = 200/4

z = 50   ⇒  cost of a notebook

Find cost of book, y:

y = z + 140

y = 50 + 140

y = 190  ⇒  cost of a book

Find the cost of a ballpen, x:

x = 130 - 2z

x = 130 - 2(50)

x = 130 - 100

x = 30  ⇒  cost of a ballpenANSWER: The cost of a notebook is Php50.

Check:

The sum of purchases in Php270:

30 + 190 + 50 = 270

270 = 270 (True)

The book cost ₱140 more than the notebook:

Book = 140 + 50

190 = 190  (True)

The book and the notebook cost ₱210 more than the ballpen.
