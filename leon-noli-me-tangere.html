8. Does Leon and his father have a better relationship with me another?​


Answer:

How My brother Leon Brought home a wife

- Manuel Arguilla

Have you heard about a provinciano who fell in love with a girl in a city? How about a girl in a city who fell in love with a provinciano? Is it really possible that love can grow between them?

At first, I don’t really understand the story, but as the story goes on, I became more interested with it. I thought Labang was a person, but he was a bull. I also imagined that Maria is a beautiful woman. Baldo is the younger brother of Leon and he was taken away by the beauty of Maria. On their way home, Maria observed that provinces are very different from the cities. It is said that the air in the province is clean, free of dust and smoke, while in cities, lots of houses, cars and noise can be observed. It was said that Maria was very afraid to meet Leon’s father because he may not like her for his son. If I were in her place, I would also feel the same way because his life is different with mine. Back to the story, Baldo didn’t bother to answer Leon’s question about why they have to pass the unusual route, because it was said that they should’ve passed through the camino real which is the main road, but they passed by the Waig or the stream. Why do you think Baldo chose to pass by the Waig than the camino real? For me, he wants to show Maria how beautiful it is to live in a province. Do you know why Maria calls Leon, Noel? Because it is his city name. When they reached the house of Leon, his father didn’t welcome them, but Baldo and his father had a little conversation about their way home. As Maria and Leon’s father talked to each other, they became comfortable and I think his father accepted Maria as his daughter-in-law.
