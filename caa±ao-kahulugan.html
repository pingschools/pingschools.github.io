29. find the value of AO


Answer:

5 cm


Step-by-step explanation:

Given that ΔRAT ~ ΔIOT

RT/TI = AT/OT


Define x:

Let x be the length of AT


Solve x:

12/9 = x/15

Cross multiply

9x = 180

Divide both sides by 9

x = 20


Find AT:

AT = x = 20 cm


Find AO

AO = AT - OT

AO = 20 - 15 = 5 cm
