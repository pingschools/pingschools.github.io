28. What is the interior angle of a heptagon


⇒To solve for the sum of the interior angles of any polygon, use the formula
                     
                        Sum = (n - 2) × 180°

⇒To solve for the interior angle of a regular polygon, use the formula:
                        
                           ∡ = Sum ÷ n

where, n=number of sides of the polygon
           ∡= interior angle

So, as per your question. A regular heptagon is a 7-sided polygon.
Therefore, n = 7 . To solve for the interior angle of a heptagon, you need to find the sum of the interior angles of a heptagon first.

Sum = (7-2) × 180°
Sum = 5 × 180°
Sum = 900°

Then you can now solve for the value of the interior angle.

∡ = Sum ÷ n
∡ = 900° ÷ 7
∡ = 128.57°

Therefore, the interior angle of a regular heptagon is 128.57°
