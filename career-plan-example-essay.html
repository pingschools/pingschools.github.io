13. 1. Why Career Planning is important. 2. Discuss the Career Planning Process. You may site example/s.​


Answer:

A career plan is important as it can help you manage the direction of your career, the job skills and knowledge you may need, how you get them and how you can secure your dream job. Developing a career plan can make major objectives that seem impossible become much more manageable.

Answer:

1. For me, Career Planning is really important for every person since it is a way to decide on their future. It helps us, especially me as a student on the field I will choose in the future.

2. Career Planning has 4 stages, each stage has it's own purpose.

a. Self-Assessment: This is the first step where you carefully asses yourself, what are your goals, what are the things that you are capable of doing, and will you be happy once you chose this field.

b. Career Exploration: In this step, you will find what career or field do you fit in. You can do some research or ask for guidance from those who are already  working in the field that you chose.

c. Career Identification: After searching for careers/fields, you should be able to end up with just one ideal occupation (for example, from the medical field, my goal is to be a successful Surgeon). This will be the start of your preparation in your chosen occupation

d. Action Plan: Lastly, you need to be prepared on the things/topics that you'll encounter to achieve your dream occupation. This may take lng since you're starting from the basics of your chosen career.

15. Discuss the components and steps in making health career plan,essay


Health career planning is very important as it guides you towards your chosen health profession. With this, there are 4 main components/steps that you need to consider as you push through your career path. The career planning process has four components: (1) Self Assessment, (2) Career Exploration, (3) Career Identification, and (4) Action Plan. Where SELF-ASSESSMENT have a thorough assessment of your self. You may start with you personality, your skills, your inclinations, your beliefs and your wishes and hopes in life. Get to know yourself better. If possible, make a checklist for your personal identification in connection to health career. And in CAREER EXPLORATION

this time you need to check on the list of health careers. Which among them suits, your personality, values, skills and preferred type of education. Also, please consider your financial and professional growth in the preferred health career. Plus in CAREER IDENTIFICATION

After carefully selecting your chosen career, know the qualifications. Start to prepare yourself, your educational status and your preferences in accordance o your chosen field. And in CREATE AN ACTION PLAN you can decide with your chosen field, start creating your action plan as a guide for your future career. Always consider time, finances. Also, please make sure that your action plan is achievable.

As I began my career planning in High school the realization of how long it would take to become a doctor discouraged me. I once again changed my career goals to becoming a teacher, and then finally to a nurse who could assist with the delivery of babies.
