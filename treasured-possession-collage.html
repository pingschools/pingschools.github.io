4. it is a unique basket made of rattan and used to store rice carry food or even store treasured possession that can be found in palawan​


Answer:

Tingkep

Explanation:

A famous handicraft produced by the Palawanos is called tingkep. It is a unique basket made of rattan and used to store rice, carry food, or even store treasured possessions. Tingkep is also used by healers during a ritual called kundu to house the spirit of Linamen, a beautiful and powerful female spirit.
