4. 2017 reaction of sona please answer


Ang SONA, na kung saan ay madalas na broadcast, ay nagsisilbing isang paraan upang ipagbigay-alam sa bansa ang kasalukuyang kondisyong pang-ekonomiya, pampulitika, at panlipunan. Ito rin ay isang sasakyan para sa Pangulo na ibuod ang mga nagawa at mga plano ng kanyang programa ng pamahalaan kapwa para sa isang partikular na taon at hanggang sa katapusan ng panahon ng kanilangkatungkulan.

Dahil sa kanyang matatag at matibay na pagkatao, naakit ni Duterte ang karamihan ng mga boto ng Pilipino ang dahilan kung bakit siya ay nakuha ang posisyon bilang pinuno ng bansa.

Bukod sa kanyang lakas, kilala rin siya sa kanyang pangako na magdala ng pagbabago sa bansang ito at sa itaas ng kanyang listahan mula pa noon ay ang kanyang layunin na wakasan ang iligal na droga sa ating bansa.

Sa SONA ngayong taon, iniulat ng presidente ang lahat ng mga plano na kanyang ginawa at ang mga plano na itinakda niya sa susunod na taon.

Pinapurihan ng aming pangulo ang kanyang pangalawang Estado ng Nation Address. Sinabi niya ang maraming mga paksa tulad ng birth control; Sinabi niya na hindi siya para sa pagpapalaglag o pagkontrol ng kapanganakan ngunit binibigyan niya ang desisyon sa mga magulang sa pagsasaalang-alang sa kanilang nais na sukat ng pamilya. Sinabi rin niya ang pagtawag sa mga mamumuhunan na magtayo ng mga pabrika upang iproseso ang mga hilaw na materyales upang makabuo ng mga produkto. Siguro dahil sa sobrang populasyon at kakulangan ng mga trabaho kaya na gusto niyang maglagay ng higit pang mga pabrika dito sa Pilipinas upang bawasan ang pagkawala ng trabaho ng ating bansa. Binanggit din niya ang mga buwis at ang mga sundalo na nakikipagdigma sa Marawi. Bilang isang estudyante sa mataas na paaralan, nakikita ko na ang pangangailangan ng ating bansa para sa mga remedyo dahil sa mga hindi inaasahang digmaan, kalamidad at kalamidad at maaari na akong magmasid kung ano ang kailangan ng ating bansa upang mabuhay at tumataas. Nagulat ako sa kanyang address dahil sa kabila ng mga bagay na nangyayari sa ating bansa, ginagawa lang niya ang kanyang sarili na mas pessimistic at medyo cool siya pagdating sa paglutas ng mga pambansang problema. Umaasa ako na ipagpapatuloy niya ang mga bagay na ginawa niya pagkatapos ng kanyang termino at patuloy na ginagawang mapayapa ang ating bansa.

30. REACTION PAPER ABOUT SONA 2017. PLS ANSWER


President Rodrigo Duterte takes his second SONA this July and delivers reports, actions taken and currently going, and future plans of His administration as it takes seat in position.    

He expresses his efforts about change, in which, according to him, should start from top to bottom, referring to government officials down below to the lowest people in the society.  He speaks about change particularly in the attitude as this is the reason why the Republic is experiencing so much problems especially with regards to poverty.    

I agree to his statement about the attitude that should be changed.  Well, attitude really matters because this defines the person we are inside, as well as the discipline inculcated in the Filipino culture.      

Again in his speech, he describes drugs as the real menace in the society.  He said that the fight is continuous because illegal drugs, according to him, is a root cause of so much evil.    

Well, I do not agree to this statement.  I think it is the love of money, not the money itself, but the love of money, which plays a large role of evil.  People greed about money and power, and so, be it illegal they push it through to gain large sum of money.  He denies the real cause of so much evil, and it is Satan the Devil who really is behind of all evils we are experiencing.  I am not sure if he is aware of this fact.    

He also affirms the declaration of Martial Law as the means to wage war against criminality and terrorism in the Mindanao region.  Again, Satan the devil is behind the criminality and acts of injustices happening anywhere in the world, and not the people doing injustices themselves.     

He encourages everyone to cooperate, even opposers of his administration.  He calls the attention of industrialists and capitalist to invest here in the Philippines and process raw materials into finished products.     

He also warned greedy mining investors to repair the environment they have once used and abused.  He said that they will be closed or taxed to death if they will not comply.     

This should be executed immediately because the problem of mining complicates other means of living especially farmers and fishers – people who are most affected by mining industries.  This problem is not new.  If the President really is not aware of this situation, then his statement and after it will be carried out is well spoken of, having praises and grades of 100!    

As to the conclusion, my reaction to SONA 2017 is good and to some extent is acceptable.  We must take note however, that whoever intellectual, powerful human takes over the seat of Presidency or whatever high position in the government, the solution does not lies in their hands.  We look forward however, to Jesus model prayer when he says “Let Your Kingdom come...in the Earth as it is in Heaven.”  I believe that it is only the Kingdom of God that could give a real change the situation here on earth.
