13. if m1 =2x-15 and m5=x+7 find the m1 and m5 in degree​


Answer:

m∠1 = 29°m∠5 = 29°

Step-by-step explanation:

Analyze:

The transversal t intersects parallel lines l₁ and l₂. The intersections formed different angles.  ∠1 and ∠5 are corresponding angles. These are angle pairs on the same side of transversal.  

Corresponding angles are congruent.

Hence:

∠1 ≅ ∠5

Given:

m∠1 = 2x - 15m∠5 = x + 7

Equate the expressions because corresponding angles are congruent.

2x - 15 = x + 7

2x - x = 7 + 15

x = 22

Substitute 22 to x:

m∠1 = 2x - 15    

m∠1 = 2(22) - 15

m∠1 = 44 - 15

m∠1 = 29°

m∠5 = x + 7

m∠5 = (22) + 7

m∠5 = 29°

18. what is the percentage by volume if 25 m1 of alcohol is added to make 50 m1 solution​?show your solution ​


Given:

[tex]\text{volume}_{solute} = \text{25 m L}[/tex]

[tex]\text{volume}_{solution} = \text{50 m L}[/tex]

Required:

[tex]\% \: \text{by volume}[/tex]

Equation:

[tex]\% \: \text{by volume} = \frac{\text{volume}_{solute}}{\text{volume}_{solution}} × 100[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]\% \: \text{by volume} = \frac{\text{25 m L}}{\text{50 m L}} × 100[/tex]
