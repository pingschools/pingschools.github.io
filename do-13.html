26. how to do mixed number the 13/3​


Answer:

To convert a fraction to a mixed number, you first need to divide the numerator by the denominator to determine the whole number part of the mixed number. In this case, if you divide 13 by 3, you get 4 with a remainder of 1. This means that the whole number part of the mixed number is 4, and the fractional part is 1/3. Therefore, the mixed number equivalent of 13/3 is 4 1/3.
