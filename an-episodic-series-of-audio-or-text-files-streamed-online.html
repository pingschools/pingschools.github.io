2. An episodic series of audio or text files streamed online


A podcast is the episodic series of audio or text files streamed online. This series can be in the form of spoken word, digital audio files that you can download on your device for easy listening and streaming. This kind of media formatting and services provide a convenient, integrated way to achieve a personal consumption queue across many podcast sources and playback devices. A podcast series normally features one or more recurring hosts involved in a discussion about a particular topic or current event. Discussion and content within a podcast can range from being scripted to totally improvised script.
