3. the curds in the sour milk consist of what major protein​


Answer:

Casein

Explanation:

The main ingredient in curd is casein, the reserve protein of milk which gives the product its white main colour. The increased acidity causes the milk proteins (casein) to tangle into curds. Milk that has been left to sour will also naturally produce curds.
