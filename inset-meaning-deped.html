14. differences between inset map and locator map​


Answer:

Traditionally, inset maps are shown at a larger scale (smaller area) than the main map. Often, an inset map is used as a locator map that shows the area of the main map in a broader, more familiar geographical frame of reference. Inset maps may also be used to show the detail of a particular area of interest on a map.

17. What is the meaning deped vision

What is the meaning of Department of Education (DEPED) Vision?

The DepEd vision means that the organization dreams of making students who have love for their country and skilled enough to contribute to their country's success.

Teachers also dream of students who are competent and prepared enough to face everyday life. It is also must be learner-centered.

What is the DepEd Mission?

To keep everyone including students, teachers and staff safe and to promote the rights of every Filipino.

What is the purpose of DepEd?

The Department of Education or DepEd is the department of the Philippine government responsible for ensuring access to, promotes equity and improve the quality of education.

What is the difference between Mission and Vision of DepEd?

A mission statement defines what DepEd is currently doing, while a vision statement is the ultimate goal of what they'd like to accomplish. The mission is what DepEd do in order to achieve the vision.

23. balik-aral deped meaning


Ang programang Balik-Aral ay tumutukoy sa pagtanggap ng mga estudyanteng nahinto sa pag-aaral nang ilang taon ngunit nais nang magpatuloy nito.

Balik-Aral learners ang tawag ng Department of Education sa mga estudyanteng nagpapalista sa pasukan. Hindi tinatanggihan ng DepEd sa enrollment ang sinumang nahinto sa pag-aaral kahit pa nang maraming taon.

Kalimitang nahihinto sa pag-aaral ang marami dahil sa kawalan ng panustos. Kabilang sa nahihinto sa pag-aaral ng mga batang napipilitang magtrabaho para makatulong sa pamilya.

Mayroon ding nahihinto sa pag-aaral dahil kapos na kapos na sila sa perang pambaon, pambili ng mga gamit sa paaralan, o kahit pa pambili ng damit o tsinelas. Maraming biktima ng kahirapan ang nais makapag-aral ngunit hindi nabibigyan ng pagkakataon dahil sa mga hamon ng buhay.

Sa tulong ng programang Balik-Aral, buong giliw na tinatanggap ang mga nais ulit makapasok sa mga paaralan. Para sa mga matatanda nang nais makapag-aral, nariyan naman ang Alternative Learning System ng DepEd.
