5. problem of dead poet society, issue being raised in the story: keating way vs. welton way SANA PO MAY MAKASAGOT KAYLANGAN KO PO KASI NGAYUN... Thank you:>


The problem of dead poet society, an issue being raised in the story: Keating way vs. Welton way is the conflict between two different teaching methods. The Welton Academy, where the story takes place, is a prestigious all-boys preparatory school that follows a traditional and conservative teaching style. Mr. Keating, a new English teacher at the school, encourages his students to think for themselves and to pursue their passions. This creates a conflict between the two teaching styles, which ultimately leads to the tragic events of the story.

Answer:

For centuries, Egyptian royalty guarded the sacred rituals that guaranteed divine favor after death, but over time all Egyptians, both rich and poor, could possess its secrets. The Book of the Dead helped Egyptians prepare for the afterlife where Osiris, god of the underworld, would judge them.

SYMBOLISM:

Dead Stars symbolizes the unspoken present things. The affection and love between Alfredo and Julia seemed to be existing and real, however, with the passage of time, it fades away like a dead star. Hence, the disillusionment and memories of the past do not exist anymore. After the eight years of reunion, the fading love was not because of the fading youth, but because Alfred finds her different from what he perceived in the past and all the gone years. He is disillusioned. The illusions that he concealed all the years turns out to be nothing but dead stars; it was dead long ago, yet it emits apparently real lights to travel the long distance.
