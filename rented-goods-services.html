13. Suppose that JM & JT Rental Services charges an initial rental fee of S85 for a vehicle. For every day that you rent a vehicle, you are charged an additional fee of S20 per day. Write an equation representing the total cost to rent a vehicle. What is the total cost if you rent a car for an eight day trip?​


[tex]\huge \underline{\tt \green{ SCIENCE(MATH)//ANSWER}}[/tex]

[tex]\huge \underline{\tt \green{ PROBLEM}}[/tex]

Suppose that JM & JT Rental Services charges an initial rental fee of S85 for a vehicle. For every day that you rent a vehicle, you are charged an additional fee of S20 per day. Write an equation representing the total cost to rent a vehicle. [tex]\small \underline{\tt \green{What \: is \: the \: total \: cost \: if \: you \: rent \: a \: car \: for \: a\: eight \: day \: trip?}}[/tex]

[tex]\huge \underline{\tt \green{ ANSWER//SOLUTION}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\underline{\boxed{\sf \normalsize \green{c = 85+20d}}}[/tex][tex]\large\underline{\boxed{\sf \normalsize \green{c = 245}}}[/tex]

Cost = initial fee + cost per day * number of days

Cost = initial fee + cost per day * number of dayscost 85 + 20*d where d is the number of days

[tex]\large\underline{\boxed{\sf \normalsize \green{c = 85+20d}}}[/tex]

We need to rent for 8 days so d = 8

c = 85+ 20*8c = 85 + 160[tex]\large\underline{\boxed{\sf \normalsize \green{c = 245}}}[/tex]
