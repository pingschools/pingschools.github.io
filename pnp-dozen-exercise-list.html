27. One dozen roses costs ₱250. half a dozen of gladiolas costs ₱125. how much do half a dozen roses and 2 dozens gladiolas cost?


Answer:

166.6 RUPEES

Explanation:

Step-by-step explanation:

12 ROSES COST 250

SO, 1 ROSE WILL COST= 250/12=20.8RS

AND, 6 GLADIOLAS COST 125 RS

SO, 1 GLADIOLA WILL COST=125/6=20.8RS

ATQ,

HALF A DOZEN ROSES WOULD COST=20.8×6=₹125

AND, 2 GLADIOLAS WOULD COST=20.8×2=₹41.6

SO, IN TOTAL COST OF HALF A DOZEN ROSES AND TWO GLADIOLAS WOULD COST=125+41.6

=₹166.6
