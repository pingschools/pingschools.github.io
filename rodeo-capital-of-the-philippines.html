30. A. Look up each of the following words in your dictionary. Write the guide words that appear on the page.1. snorkel2. obtuse3. gesture4. prognosis5. dialectB. Write the origin (etymology) of each word. Use the dictionary.1. prairie2. license3. rodeo4. guitar5. plaid


Answer:

1. Prairie: from Latin prātum ‘meadow’.

2. License: from Old French licens ‘liberty’, from Latin licentia, from licēre ‘to be allowed’.

3. Rodeo: from Spanish rodear ‘to go around’, extended form rodear 'to surround'.

4. Guitar: from French guitare, from Spanish guitarra ‘stringed instrument’, diminutive of cithara, from Latin cithara.

5. Plaid: from Scottish Gaelic plaide ‘blanket’, related to Welsh plaidd ‘tartan’.
