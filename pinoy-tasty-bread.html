17. Do you think the demand for pinoy pandesal and pinky tasty is elastic? What do you think will happen to the demand for pandesal and tasty when prices increase by 15% and by 100%?


Without equation we cannot guarantee for sure by how much. However we can say that the demand for pinoy pandesal and pinoy tasty is relatively elastic. We can say that it is elastic because elasticity represents the "reactiveness" of the demand to the changes of price in the product. We say relatively because if we increase the price by 15%, buyers could still take the price and buy as many pandesal as they have been buying in the past however if we increase the price by 100% there might already be some cuts in the demand for pandesal and tasty.

27. Do you think the demand for pinoy pandesal and pinoy tasty is elastic? what do you think will happen to the demand for pandesal and tasty when price increase by 15% and by 100%?


The increase of 15% in the price of Pinoy Pandesal and Pinoy Tasty would be considered Inelastic since it is a regular part of the Filipino breakfast, such a small increase would not be felt by the patrons,they may buy less of it but the demand would still be there.

If a store or a bakery increases the price of both types of bread by 100%, the demand may become Elastic. With such a big jump in its price, people may choose to have rice, taho, champorado and others for breakfast to save money.

We can not be sure about how people would actually react to the given scenarios. From my personal observation, people buy pandesal everyday in such small amounts that in reality a hungry tricycle or jeepney driver on the go may continue to buy them daily at small amounts. The pinoy tasty is a different situation, being packaged and staying within a fixed weight, the 100% increase will most certainly drive off money wise consumers.

Even without the actual increase and decrease in sales figures to get the exact answer, we can tell how consumers would react when it comes to these products given the two situations above.
