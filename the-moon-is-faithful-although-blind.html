3. The moon is faithful, although blind what technique is being used


Answer:

The literary technique used in the given example is personification.

Personification is the literary technique whereby human attributes are given to inanimate objects

Some examples include:

The moon smiled on us

The wood had a fiery temper

The fan blew happily

Therefore, the sentence, "The moon is faithful, though blind" is personification because the moon is inanimate and therefore cannot be faithful, which is a human attribute. Also, the moon cannot be blind as that is also a human attribute
