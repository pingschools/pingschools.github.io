8. What is a appropriate attire for males in modern standard dance


What is a appropriate attire for males in modern standard dance

It is coat and tie.

The dress requirements are the same for all of these dances. The clothes will suit the so-called "white tie" dress code: a full dress for women and a bow tie and a men's tail coat. There are, however, several variations between traditional dresses and tailcoats and their dance variants.  The dress code allows the dancer to put all the shirt buttons on, carefully tuck them in, rolled up sleeves are not permitted. A shirt must be worn with a bow tie; extra accessories such as luxury cufflinks or shirt studs will improve the elegance of your outfit. The dance shirt is not really a top, even though it looks like one. A dance shirt is a leotard with a shorts on the bottom or straps around between the thighs. Such an method means that the shirt will still look good and remain straight, not scrunch up, even though you make complicated dance moves.
