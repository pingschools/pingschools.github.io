23. Answer the following briefly: 1. What is one of the traditional game in the Philippines which uses slippers or can as pamato? 2. What is the objective of this game? 3. What is called to the guard of the can? 4. Where will the guard placed the can? 5. What are the things needed for this game?​


Answer:

1. Tumbang preso or presohan (tumba-patis in most Visayan regions) is a popular Filipino street game and is commonly seen in most Filipino movies and TV series.

2. The objective is for the players to hit and knock down the can with the slipper, and for the tayà to put back the can inside a small circle a few meters away from the toe-line.

3. One player or the tayà (the "It") guards the can

4. Tayà put back the can inside a small circle a few meters away from the toe-line.

5. The equipment needed is an empty soda can or any kind of can or bottle, and a slipper for each player. To make the game enjoyable and exciting, there should be no more than nine players.

Answer:

1. Tumbang Preso

2. The objective is for the players to hit and knock down the can with the slipper.

3. In tumbang preso the player who guards the tin can called the "it" or in Filipino, the "Taya".

4. The guard will put the can inside a small circle a few meters away from the toe-line.

5. The equipment needed is an empty soda can or any kind of can or bottle, and a slipper for each player. To make the game enjoyable and exciting, there should be no more than nine players.
