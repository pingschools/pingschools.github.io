Biosphere: The biosphere is a global ecosystem composed of living organisms (biotic) and the abiotic (nonliving) factors from which they derive  energy and nutrients.

Ecosystem: An ecosystem is a geographic area where plants, animals, and other organisms, as well as weather and landscape, work together to form a bubble of life.

Community: A group of people with diverse characteristics who are linked by social ties, share common perspectives, and engage in joint action and are made up of population.

Population: A group of individuals of the same species living and interbreeding within a given area making a community.

Organism: A living thing that has an organized structure, can react to stimuli, reproduce, grow, adapt.

↳ THE DIFFERENT LEVELS OF A BIOLOGICAL ORGANIZATION AND ITS DEFINITION ༉‧₊˚✧AtomAn atom is the start of everything in the universe. It is the smallest unit matter that builds up other matters such as a molecule. MoleculeA molecule is a chemical structure of two or more atoms that are chemically bonded. OrganelleWhen molecules/ macromolecules are combined together, it will form an organelle which is also known as “Tiny Organs”. Cell When organelles are bonded together, it will form a cell that is the basic unit of life. A cell forms a living organism's structure and functions.

TissueTissues are made out of similar cells that are working together to perform a particular function. An organ is a group of tissues. OrganOrgans work together for an organism or a living thing to function -  forming an organ system. OrganismAn organism is the living things that may, or may not live together with other living organisms. Population An organism living with other organisms that are identical to itself is called a  population. CommunityWhen other organism’s population works and lives together with other organisms - forms a community. EcosystemWhen a community of living things live together with non-living things to survive, we call it an ecosystem. BiosphereThere are different kinds of ecosystems in both land, water, and air. With different kinds of ecosystem present, it forms the biosphere, a.k.a, our planet.
