28. In running the business, why is it important to familiarize and study first the 3s of opportunity spotting and assessment ?​


AnswerFirst, what are these 3S of opportunity spotting and assessment?​

S1: Seeking the opportunity

It is the process of developing new ideas from several sources.

S2: Screening the opportunity

It is the process of carefully choosing the best opportunity

S3: Seizing the opportunity

It is the phase where the Entrepreneurs execute the plans and 'grabs the opportunity' after thoroughly seeking and screening.

Hence, the 3S is important for an Entrepreneur, because, it is a feasibility study for an effective product or service for a particular opportunity. An opportunity, in this context, pertains to a business idea that has the potential to become a feasible product or service.
