30. quinton bought x number of shares for p dollars and paid 1% commission. he sold the stock for y dollars and paid a flat fee of $7. express quinton's net proceeds algebraically


Answer:

Y - (0.01xp + 7)

Step-by-step explanation:

As per the statement:

Quinton bought x number of shares for p dollars and paid a 1% commission

⇒He  brought = 0.01xp

It is also given that he sold the stock for y dollars and paid a flat fee of $7.

⇒He sold = y - 7

then;

Net proceed is given as:

Y - 7 - 0.01xp = Y - (0.01xp + 7)

Therefore,  quintons net proceeds algebraically is

Y - (0.01xp + 7)
