5. components of a torch circuit


Answer:

1:Power source: This could be a battery, wall outlet, or other source of electricity.

2:Switch: This component allows you to turn the circuit on and off.

3:Fuse: This is a safety device that protects the circuit from overheating or short-circuiting.

4:Resistor: This component limits the flow of electricity through the circuit, helping to prevent overheating and damage.

5:Bulb: The bulb is the component that produces light when electricity flows through it.

6:Reflector: This component is used to direct and focus the light produced by the bulb.

7:Housing: This is the physical structure that holds all of the other components together and allows you to hold or carry the torch.

22. what is function in welding torch​


Function in welding torch

A welding torch is used in an automatic welding system to direct the welding electrode into the arc, to conduct welding power to the electrode, and to provide shielding of the arc area. The major function of the torch is to deliver the welding current to the electrode.
