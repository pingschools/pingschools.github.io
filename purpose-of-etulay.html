1. purpose of etulay program


Answer:

ETULay is a free online tutorial platform for learners given by excellent and inspiring educators, who assume the role of a facilitator and guide the students to another level of learning experience. This online tutorial will also help parents to guide the studies of their children right at the comfort of their homes.


2. purpose of etulay program


Answer:

Dubbed “ETULay,” the new learning platform aims to bridge the gaps and improve the quality of learning under the distance learning setup currently implemented this school year amid the continued threat of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19).

6. newest etulay program offering


Answer:

As we know we are faced now in the new trend of learning, as a result of this situation the government tries to support all the needs speacially education. Through the collaboration of our dedicated teachers, they launched an educational program that can assist the school children.

What is ETUlay ?  

Answer: D    

    The project, called "ETULay," aims to close gaps and enhance education quality in the remote learning environment as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic, which has produced a new normal of schooling

     The Department of Education has introduced a free online tutoring network facilitated by expert educators to assist students and parents in navigating distance learning from home (DepEd). With city government policies ensure that no student falls behind in the modern normal of education.

    “We must find innovative ways to modernize our educational system, which will necessitate upgrading learning delivery through the complete and imaginative implementation of technology in the digital age. “This ETULay initiative is only one of several steps DepEd is taking to ensure that this transition occurs for the good of our students,” Secretary Leonor Magtolis Briones said.

         

      The lessons are presented in a tutoring style, with instructions on how to complete exercises and explanations of the lesson's details. ETULay was also developed around the weekly Most Important Learning Competencies (MELCs) to make it easier for parents and students to respond to Self-Learning Modules modeled after Region IV-PIVOT A's 4A QuBE program.

      For a more inclusive learning experience for all Filipinos, the DepEd curriculum includes sessions on Storytelling, Fitness, Mother Tongue-Based Multilingual Education (MTB-MLE), English, Filipino, Mathematics, Edukasyon sa Pagpapakatao (EsP), and Health

   Top broadcast journalists are planning Teacher-Broadcasters for the Department of Education's (DepEd) TV productions, which will act as the "new standard" learning platform for students who do not have access to the internet.

7. which of the following is the newest etulay online tutorial program offering?


Answer:

ETULAY online tutorials were conceptualized on 04 January 2021 to support the different blended learning modalities of DepEd. The courses are contained in a tutorial web format with the following objectives: To guide learners in answering Self-Learning Modules (SLMs);

Headed by ICTS-EdTech Unit Head Mark Anthony Sy at the start of 2021, the initiative's goals are: (1) to empower and give the parents full confidence in assisting their children, (2) help learners to fully understand the lesson, and (3) help teachers in explaining the lesson.

ETULAY combines the acronym for Educational Technology Unit (ETU) and tulay,' the Filipino term for bridge. ETULAY literarily means 'bridging' and DepEd has coined this term to refer to its online courses
